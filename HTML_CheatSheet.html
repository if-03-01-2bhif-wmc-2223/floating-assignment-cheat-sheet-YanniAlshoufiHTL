Image Formats

JP(E)G

no motion, photos, compression, good res, transparent background not possible, light, "blurry" at certein spots in some cases

GIF

motion possible, graphics and photos, low quality, 256 colors per bit, transparent possible, color stored as a number, light

PNG

no motion, photos, good quality, looseless, transparent/translucent background possible, big sizes possible

ICO

no motion, graphs & icons, low quality, transparent background possible, very light

SVG

no motion, graphs via vector graphs, transparent background possible, very light, hight quality

mpg and mp4

mainly for videos (motion), moving pictures, lossy (like jpeg), mostly light (compared to raw video), transparent background possible
